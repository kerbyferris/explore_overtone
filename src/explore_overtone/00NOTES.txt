Notes and Aural & Temporal Spaces

quasi-pitch?
  - C (frequencyless?)
    - tonic,scale,index - C,major,0

pitch
  - frequency (Hz)
    - midi-pitch (floating-point)
      - midi-pitch-index C4 (integer)
        - tonic,scale,index - C4,major,0
time (+ durations)
  - real-time (s) = relative to some system time
    - sequence-time (s) = relative to start of sequence
      - sequence-beat = tempo() + beat-index, relative to start of sequence
